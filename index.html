The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future for world.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Yunus's core theory revolves around the idea that providing very small loans to impoverished individuals, particularly women, can empower them to start their own businesses and lift themselves out of poverty. Traditional banks often deny loans to the poor due to perceived high risk and lack of collateral. Yunus recognized the inherent entrepreneurial spirit and creditworthiness of the poor and developed a system to provide them with access to capital.
